About Me

I am currently a 3rd year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I am also a Microsoft Research Asia Fellow. I work with Prof. Kai Chen in System networkING (SING) research group. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Information Security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2013.

I am broadly interested in computer networking with a special focus on datacenter networking. I try to understand and improve network performance by building real systems. I enjoy solving real world problems in a simple and practical style (so-called 'dirty-slate' works). But I don't like (also not good at) writing papers. Actually, I define myself as a junior network developer. I am lucky to work with my advisor Prof. Kai Chen (who helps me write so many exciting papers) and many outstanding collaborators: Dr. Haitao Wu (Microsoft Azure), Prof. Dongsu Han (KAIST), and Prof. Chen Tian (NJU).
